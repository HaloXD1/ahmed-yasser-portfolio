# Referral Outreach System

Goal: stop applying silently. Each strong application should have a human touchpoint.

## Daily Workflow

1. Pick 2 roles from `application_tracker.csv`.
2. Apply with the best CV version.
3. Find 1 recruiter, talent person, alumnus, or junior employee.
4. Send one short message.
5. Set follow-up date 5-7 days later.
6. Update tracker status.

## Contact Priority

1. University alumni at the company.
2. Junior data engineer / BI analyst / cloud engineer.
3. Recruiter or talent acquisition.
4. Team lead or manager only when no better contact exists.

## Message Rules

- Keep it under 90 words.
- Mention one project only.
- Ask for guidance, not a job.
- Do not sound desperate.
- Do not send long paragraphs.

## LinkedIn Connection

Hi [Name], I am Ahmed, a Business Informatics student applying for Summer 2026 data/BI internships. I built Python/SQL projects with ETL, data validation, dashboards, CI, and Docker. I saw your work at [Company] and would be grateful to connect.

## After Applying

Hi [Name], I recently applied for [Role] at [Company]. My strongest fit is Python/SQL data pipeline work; my retail ETL project includes data contracts, incremental loading, KPI exports, tests, Docker, and CI. If you know the best next step or team contact, I would appreciate your guidance.

## Referral Ask After They Reply

Thank you. If you think my background is relevant, would you be comfortable referring me or pointing me to the right application channel? No pressure either way.
